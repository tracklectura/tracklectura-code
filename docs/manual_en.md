# 📖 User Manual — TrackLectura

## What is TrackLectura?

TrackLectura is a desktop application for logging and analyzing your reading habits. You can keep a detailed record of each reading session: pages read, time spent, and reading speed — all synchronized to the cloud.

---

## Login

When you open the app, you will see the login screen. You have three options:

- **Iniciar sesión** (Login) — enter your email and password if you already have an account.
- **Registrarse** (Sign up) — create a new account with your email and password. You will receive a verification email; confirm your account before logging in.
- **Acceder sin conexión / Modo Invitado** (Guest mode) — access the app without an internet connection. Data is saved locally on your device only and will not be synced.

---

## Main screen

Once logged in, you will see the main screen with the following sections:

### 📚 Book selection
- Type in the search field to filter your books, or click **▼** to see the full list.
- When you select a book, the start page is automatically filled with the last page you read.

### ➕ Book management
- **➕ Añadir Nuevo Libro** (Add New Book) — enter the title and the total number of pages (optional).
- **...** (Edit) — update the total page count of an existing book.
- **Eliminar libro seleccionado** (Delete selected book) — removes the book and all its sessions. This action cannot be undone.

### 🖼️ Book cover
- The cover is searched automatically when you select a book.
- If the cover is not correct, use **↻** (other cover) to browse other options and **✓** (correct) to confirm your choice.
- Use **Subir Portada** (Upload Cover) to choose an image from your device.

### 📌 Book status
You can mark a book as: **Por leer** (To read), **Leyendo** (Reading), **Terminado** (Finished) or **Abandonado** (Abandoned).

### 🔥 Streak and daily goal
- The top right shows your daily reading progress and your streak of consecutive reading days.
- Click on the streak label to change your daily page goal.

---

## Recording a reading session

1. Select the book you are going to read.
2. Enter the **Capítulo / Relato** (chapter or story) — optional.
3. Check the **Página inicio** (start page) — filled automatically.
4. Press **▶ INICIAR** (Start) to begin the timer.
5. Read normally. You can pause with **⏸ PAUSAR** (Pause) and resume with **▶ INICIAR** (Start).
6. When you finish, enter the **Página fin** (end page) and press **⏹ TERMINAR** (Stop).
7. The app will automatically calculate the pages read, your reading speed (PPM and PPH), and display a session summary.

---

## Statistics and charts

Press **📊 VER PROGRESO** (View Progress) to open the statistics window, where you can see:
- Charts of speed, pages read and progress per session.
- Overall stats: average speed, longest session, most productive day, etc.
- Estimated time remaining to finish the book.
- Export data to **CSV** or **PNG image**.

---

## Session history and editing

Press **📜 EDITAR REGISTROS** (Edit Records) to open the history for the selected book. From here you can:
- View all recorded sessions.
- **➕ Añadir Sesión** (Add session) — add a session manually.
- **✏️ Editar Seleccionada** (Edit selected) — edit an existing session (chapter, pages, time).
- **🗑️ Eliminar Seleccionada** (Delete selected) — delete a session.

---

## Light / dark theme

Press the **🌙** (dark mode) / **☀️** (light mode) button in the top right corner to toggle between themes. Your preference is saved automatically.

---

## Cloud synchronization

Data is automatically synchronized with the cloud when you open and close the application. In guest mode, data is only saved locally on your device.
